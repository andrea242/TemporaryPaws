About Us

Temporary Paws was established in 2016.It it a community formed for dog lovers and dog enthusiasts.We wanted to make life easier for anyone looking to borrow or rent out their dogs.We know how hard it is for lenders to find out if their dog is in good care,which is why Temporary paws takes all the hassle and the concern out of your mind.

Our Values

At Temporary Paws we look for dog enthusiasts because we belive that dogs should be looked after and cared for with the utmost care.We believe that a dog is not just a dog,it is part of your life.

We want dog enthusiasts to interact with one and other,it is not just for owners with dogs,it is for anyone that loves dogs.This is why we have proudly created this community known as Temporary Paws.

Our Mission

Temporary Paws has created this community with a vision to connect all dog enthuasists together.We know some dog owners just do not have the time to care for their dog due to work commitments etc.At the same time we have hundreds of dog lovers who will care for your dog as their own.

We want you to know that your dog is being fed,walked and cared for by an avid dog enthuasist.

Our Vison

To do the best we can to unite all dog enthusiasts
